Educating Young Minds March 2019

Literacy Training Service

Barangay 250, Zone 23, District II

NSTP is a requirements for a non-foreign student in the University. It includes cooperating with communities that are in need of assistance. Teaching the students in a poor community basic lessons has been included to create a structure for the community. We are tasked to teach students basic hygiene and so the group decided to include a little donation of hygiene kits that can be a stepping stone towards a community that entirely practices good hygiene.
