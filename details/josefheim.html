Instituition Visit

    

Visit and serve the elderly

Josefheim Foundation Inc. is location at 6 Riverside Dr., Provident Village, Marikina City. The foundation started in September 2015 through the guidance of Fr. Dari Dioquino, the head of the foundation. This is where the students did the institutional visit. The name of "Josefheim" is a German term which means "Home". Hence, the house of Joseph. Currently, the institution is handling 12 abandoned patients from the Hospital of Quirino and Amang Rodriguez. Ages of the elderly living here are from 37 to 91 years old and are being assisted and taken care of by five staffs of Fr. Dari. The institution aims to provide the elderly the highest quality of life that they might have failed to experience in their past years. The institution strive to serve and give proper care to the elderly.
