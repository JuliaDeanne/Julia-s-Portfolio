Fieldwork

    

Asuncion Consunji Elementary School, Samal, Bataan

The Natonal Service Traning Program - Literacy Training Service implemented its two-Saturday fieldwork activities on the second semester of the year 2018-2019. The two-Saturday fieldwork activity is part of the NSTP/LTS wherein the students are deployed in several parts of the communities to train into teaching the school children literacy and basic numerical skills. The project was held at Asuncion Consunji Elementary School, Samal, Bataan. The conducted fieldwork project is about Lakbay Turo Tutorial and it's about helping and teaching students in the field of Mathematics and English. In mathematics, the group have taught the learners on how to solve mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. While the English subjecy focuses on how to pronounce English words correctly, its definition and translation in Filipino and the basic parts of speech such as nouns, verbs and adjectives.
